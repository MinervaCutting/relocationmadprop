Canalla Bistró

This restaurant is located inside a very special space called Platea Madrid. It is the largest gastro leisure space in Europe.

Platea is a unique gastronomic leisure space. A place to relax , enjoy yourself and meet friends in a setting where everything comes together.

Platea boasts almost 6000m2 arranged over two floors and three galleries. It has different gastronomic spaces: El Foso that offers food from around the world and patisserie bar. El Patio that is a tapas bar . El Palco Cocktail & Bar
